Have you seen this?

The most common way of creating and deploying software often leads to cost overruns, usability issues, unmitigated risks, and overall fragility.

We can do better.

I want IT teams to rise above that. I want the businesses that rely on them to be more successful, more flexible, more resilient, and ultimately, more profitable.

Easily.

It turns out that all of this is easily within reach of even the smallest groups of developers, engineers, and support staff, including teams of just 1!

Experience

Having been involved in the software development industry for over 20 years, and having been part of numerous teams, I am well aware of how software is typically built, tested, and deployed.

I've also learned that the "typical" way is far from the best.

It inevitably leads to unnecessary mistakes, unmet expectations, and high levels of stress. It also prevents the business from quickly trying new ideas, switching to a new direction, and returning the most value from their most important investment:

Their IT team.
